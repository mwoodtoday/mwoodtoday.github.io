How Can We Help You

Family Law

Our team of lawyers have the requisite experience to provide legal assistance in the area of family law particularly adoption, divorce, child custody, administration of estate, drafting of wills, amendment and revocation of wills.

Personal Injury

Our Team Of Lawyers are ready to provides legal services to those who claim to have been injured, physically or psychologically, as a result of the negligence of another person, company, government agency or any entity.

Business Law

Starting a new business, acquiring an existing one, or when dealing with issues that arise in connection with the operation and ownership of a business. The attorneys of Michael Wood Chambers, have extensive experience in business, commercial, and corporate matters.

Criminal Law

Have you been charged with a crime? You need a competent criminal attorney to take your case. The team at Michael Wood Chambers has over 20 years of criminal law experience. Receive dependable legal assistance today by scheduling an appointment with us.

Education Law

The attorneys at Michael Wood Chambers are passionate about student advocacy. When bureaucracies fail in their mission, whether through mistake, inadvertence or otherwise, an education lawyer can ensure students do not get left behind.

Real Estate Law

our real estate lawyers help close hundreds of transactions every year for clients all over the world. We support our clients throughout the course of the transaction and provide prompt and professional legal advice on a wide range of real estate law matters.
