FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What security measures does At Integrated Vaults employ to ensure the safety of precious metals and other valuables stored in safety deposit boxes?

At Integrated Vaults utilises a multi-layered security approach to ensure the utmost protection for your precious metals and other valuables :

  • High-Security Facility: Our vault boasts reinforced concrete walls, restricted access points, and 24/7 surveillance with advanced camera systems.
  • Advanced Alarm Systems: Sophisticated alarm systems are strategically placed throughout the facility and monitored constantly by a team of security professionals.
  • Rigorous Access Control: Only authorised personnel can access the vault; each entry is strictly monitored and documented.
  • Dual-Key Access: Our safety deposit boxes require two keys for access, ensuring an extra layer of security.
Can clients access their valuables stored in At Integrated Vaults’ safety deposit boxes at any time?

We offer flexible access options to cater to your needs. Standard business hours access is included with every rental agreement. You can conveniently access your valuables anytime through our online appointment scheduling.

What insurance coverage does At Integrated Vaults provide for value stored in safety deposit boxes?

While we take every precaution to ensure the safety of your valuables, At Integrated Vaults recommends that you obtain personal insurance for your gold and silver bullions, diamonds, jewellery and other valuable holdings. We can provide you with details on recommended insurance providers upon request.

Are there any restrictions on the types or quantities of gold and silver bullion that can be stored in At Integrated Vaults’ safety deposit boxes?

At Integrated Vaults generally don’t restrict the types of gold and silver bullion you can store (bars, coins, etc.). However, there may be limitations on the total weight stored in a single safety deposit box. Contact us to discuss your needs and ensure we have a box accommodating your gold bullion holdings.

Does At Integrated Vaults offer additional services or amenities for clients storing precious metals, such as auditing or authentication?

How does At Integrated Vaults handle confidentiality and privacy concerns for clients storing precious metals in safety deposit boxes?
